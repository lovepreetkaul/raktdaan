Introduction to Raktdaan

Welcome to Raktdaan.com, a free and open access database bridging the vast gap in potential donors and people in need of blood.

Scientists have not (yet) found a way of synthesising blood, so the only way this huge demand for blood can be met is through large number of voluntary donations.

The purpose of this website is to be the link that connects the donors to the needy, efficiently and before its too late

The Blood you donate may help:

  • A person who was in an accident and has lost a lot of blood
  • Anemic or pregnant women
  • Other people who have blood related diseases or blood component deficiencies

So your blood can be the difference between life and death of a fellow being.It Matters, So please donate blood and join us in this noble cause.
